WHO ARE WE?

Earthrise is not just a nonprofit organization; it's a beacon of change, a movement fueled by passion. It is a community brought together by a shared target.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We're not just another group of students; we're a force for change committed to tackling one of the most important problems of our time. Climate change isn't just a distant threat - it's happening now, and it's up to us to confront it head-on.

WHY ARE WE DOING THIS?

We want to prove that age is no barrier to making a difference. We sincerely believe that youth have the power to change the world for the good of the entire planet, so we aim to utilize this power.

HOW ARE WE GOING TO DO THIS?

We aim to motivate, inform, and empower society to contribute significantly to sustainability and conservation. We are a student-led revolution bringing about change at all levels. Through our podcasts, we dive deep into pressing environmental topics.
